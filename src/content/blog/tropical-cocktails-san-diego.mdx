San Diego has always been a beer city. But the cocktail scene has matured significantly over the past several years, and nowhere is that more visible than in the tropical and rum-forward programs that have taken hold in beach neighborhoods.

## Miss B's Coconut Club — Mission Beach

Miss B's Coconut Club is the benchmark for tropical cocktails in San Diego. The flamingo vessel, the 24 copper draft taps, the Caribbean-rooted drink program — every cocktail is built with intention. The Caribe Welcome, the Mango Guava Spritz, and the Rum Cannonball are the signatures. Bayou Hour runs on weekday afternoons.

## The Bar Program

Miss B's approach to tropical cocktails is rooted in the Caribbean tradition — fresh ingredients, quality spirits, and presentations that make every drink feel like a destination. The flamingo tap system is the visual centerpiece; the cocktail menu is what keeps people coming back.

## Where to Find It

Miss B's Coconut Club is located at 3704 Mission Blvd in Mission Beach, San Diego. Open daily — bar from 11am weekdays, 10am weekends during brunch service.
