San Diego is one of the top bachelorette destinations on the West Coast — the weather, the beach access, the bar scene, and the short flight from most major California cities make it a natural choice. The venue question is where most groups get stuck.

## What Makes a Great Bachelorette Venue

The best bachelorette venues have three things: a space that photographs well, a cocktail program worth celebrating with, and an events team that handles the logistics so the group can focus on the celebration. Miss B's Coconut Club in Mission Beach delivers all three.

## Miss B's Coconut Club for Bachelorettes

The open-air bar, the copper flamingo taps, the pink fringed umbrellas on the patio, the "In Love With The Coco" sign — Miss B's was practically designed for a bachelorette. The tropical cocktail program, the beachside location, and the full private event capabilities make it the top pick for Mission Beach celebrations.

## How to Book a Bachelorette at Miss B's

Submit a private events inquiry at missbcoconutclub.com/private-events. The team will put together a custom proposal with reserved section options, cocktail packages, and pricing. Recommend booking 4–6 weeks in advance for weekend dates.
