Mission Beach is one of San Diego's most sought-after event neighborhoods — the combination of beach access, walkable streets, and the Pacific Ocean backdrop makes it a natural choice for any celebration. But not every venue in the area is worth the booking.

## What to Look for in a Mission Beach Event Venue

The best private event venues offer a genuinely private space, a kitchen and bar program capable of executing at scale, and an events team that handles the logistics. In Mission Beach, Miss B's Coconut Club is the only venue that delivers all three with a space that's also genuinely memorable.

## Miss B's Coconut Club for Private Events

Miss B's offers three configurable spaces — the open-air bar area, the tropical dining room, and the full outdoor patio with pink fringed umbrellas. Full buyouts accommodate up to 80 guests. The events team builds custom menus and cocktail packages for every event.
