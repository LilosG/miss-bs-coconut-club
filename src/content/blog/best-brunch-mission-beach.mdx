Mission Beach is one of San Diego's most visited neighborhoods — which means its brunch scene gets a lot of foot traffic and a lot of competition. Most spots lean on the location and charge accordingly. Miss B's Coconut Club is the exception: a venue that would earn its reputation regardless of where it sat.

## What Makes Miss B's the Best Brunch in Mission Beach

The answer is the combination. The open-air teal bar. The tropical dining room with the original mural. The pink umbrella patio. The full kitchen firing from 10am. The copper flamingo taps. Bottomless mimosas available. A menu built for a long Saturday afternoon rather than a 45-minute table turn.

## What to Order

The Coconut Club Burger is the signature — a properly built burger that holds its own against any in the city. The Ahi Poke is a brunch staple. For cocktails, the Mango Guava Spritz is the morning order, and bottomless mimosas remain the most efficient path to a perfect weekend.

## When to Go

Brunch runs every Saturday and Sunday from 10am to 3pm at 3704 Mission Blvd in Mission Beach. Reservations are strongly recommended — walk-ins are welcome at the bar but guaranteed seating requires a booking, especially on Sunday mornings.
