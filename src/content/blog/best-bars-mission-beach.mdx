Mission Beach is a neighborhood built for being outside with a drink in your hand. The boardwalk, the bay, the Pacific — it's the backdrop that makes any bar better. But some bars are worth coming to specifically, not just for the setting.

## Miss B's Coconut Club — The Standard

Miss B's Coconut Club at 3704 Mission Blvd is the bar that sets the standard for Mission Beach. The open-air teal bar, the 24 copper flamingo taps, the tropical dining room and patio — it's a venue with a genuine point of view. The cocktail program is serious, the food is worth ordering, and the atmosphere is unlike anywhere else on Mission Blvd.

## The Full Bar Program

The Miss B's bar program is built on tropical craft cocktails and a 24-tap flamingo draft system. The Caribe Welcome, the Mango Guava Spritz, the Rum Cannonball — every drink has a name and a story. Open daily from 11am on weekdays and 10am on weekends.

## How to Visit

Walk-ins are always welcome at the bar. For weekend brunch or dining, reservations are recommended. Located at 3704 Mission Blvd, Mission Beach, San Diego, CA 92109.
